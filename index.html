Hello and welcome to my site.

My name is Nadia Starovoitova

They say, I was born with a pencil in my hand. Drawing has become my way of life, and drawing outfit for girls become my profession! I was educated and qualified as a fashion designer. However, I never stopped painting. For many years the costume and the allegorical female image were the main theme of my art. My favorite technique is watercolor, and I devoted my whole life to mastering the secrets and skills of watercolor painting. By meantime I am fascinated by everything that is used for drawing, such as gouache, acrylic, pastel, colored pencils and pen. Diversity of the world, the incomprehensible beauty of nature, animals, birds, flowers, water sparkling and lush green trees are mirrored in my paintings and drawings. Over years I painted many pictures, and a lot of positive comments on my works inspired me to start the on-line business and to open a store. I will be happy to get your requests and to draw or paint your pets, favorite animals, flowers and landscapes. I can also create unique congratulation cards and other things. Anything that would please you and your family! If you like to please your friends and the loved ones, try my art!
